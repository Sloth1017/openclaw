## THE DEFINING THEME OF 2026: INTENTIONALITY

The biggest trend for 2026 is **intentionality** — couples are moving away from cookie-cutter weddings and viral aesthetics toward celebrations that truly reflect who they are. As wedding planner Jove Meyer says: *"Weddings rooted in assumed traditions, binary language, or one-size-fits-all cultural frameworks are no longer resonating. The future is about creating celebrations that consciously reflect who is actually in the room."*

**What this means for you:** Your industrial chic Amsterdam wedding is perfectly on-trend — it's authentic, personal, and breaks from traditional ballroom expectations.

## TOP 2026 WEDDING THEMES

### 1. Industrial Elegance (YOUR STYLE!) ⭐
**Status:** MAJOR TREND for 2026

## FLORAL TRENDS 2026

### What's In
- **Sculptural installations** — art pieces, not just arrangements
- **Dried & preserved flowers** — sustainable, lasting
- **Unexpected color combinations** — bold and artistic
- **Local & seasonal** — Dutch tulips in spring, wildflowers in summer
- **Living walls & hanging installations** — immersive greenery
- **Minimalist bouquets** — less is more

### For Industrial Venues
- **Contrast is key:** Soft, organic florals against hard industrial lines
- **Hanging installations:** From exposed beams and high ceilings
- **Greenery walls:** To soften concrete and brick
- **Single-stem statements:** Minimalist but impactful

**Amsterdam-Specific:**
- September: Dahlias, cosmos, late sunflowers
- Dutch-grown flowers (sustainable, local)
- Consider potted plants guests can take home

## EUROPEAN WEDDING SPECIFICS

### What's Different in Europe vs. US
- **Longer dinners** — Multi-course meals, not just buffet
- **Later starts** — Weddings often start at 4-5pm, party until 3am
- **More formal dress codes** — But this is changing
- **Stronger focus on food & wine** — Culinary experience matters
- **Historic venues prized** — Castles, warehouses, unique spaces

### Dutch Wedding Trends
- **Casual elegance** — Less formal than UK, more than US
- **Gezelligheid** — Cozy, convivial atmosphere
- **Outdoor ceremonies** — When weather permits
- **Focus on food** — Dutch cheese, herring, stroopwafels
- **Bike elements** — Very Dutch, very trendy
