## 🚤 KEY VENUES WITH PRIVATE BOAT JETTIES

### **KOEPELKERK AMSTERDAM** ⭐ HIGHLY RECOMMENDED
**Location:** Central Amsterdam (Singel Canal)  
**Style:** Historic dome church + waterfront access  
**Website:** koepelkerk.com

#### Key Features:
- ✅ **Private jetty** — Guests arrive by boat
- ✅ Historic Dome Hall for ceremony
- ✅ Connected to Renaissance Amsterdam Hotel (reception space)
- ✅ Multiple room options (upper rooms)
- ✅ Central location
- ✅ Waterfront views

**Capacity:** 100-200+ (flexible with hotel spaces)  
**Vibe:** Historic + modern, elegant, formal  
**Perfect for:** Ceremony by boat → reception at hotel

**Contact:** koepelkerk.com  
**Cost Estimate:** Premium (historic + central location)

---

### **PLLEK** (Noord Waterfront)
**Location:** Amsterdam Noord (waterfront, industrial area)  
**Style:** Industrial restaurant + event space + private beach  
**Website:** pllek.nl

#### Key Features:
- ✅ **Industrial look** with waterfront terrace
- ✅ Large terrace overlooking water
- ✅ **Private beach** (unique!)
- ✅ Restaurant integrated
- ✅ Sunset views + Amsterdam skyline
- ✅ Water-adjacent location
- ✅ Boat-accessible (Noord area)

**Contact:** pllek.nl

## 💡 Boat Access Strategy

**Option A: Arrive by Boat, Party on Land**
- Guests take private boats or water taxi to venue jetty
- Ceremony/reception at waterfront restaurant
- Example: Koepelkerk or Pllek

**Option B: Entire Wedding on Water**
- Ceremony + dinner + dancing on luxury boat
- Cruises through Amsterdam canals
- More intimate (max 85 guests)
- Example: Boat Amsterdam, Smidtje

**Option C: Hybrid (Hangar Model)**
- Venue has ferry access (IJ-plein = 5 min ferry ride from city center)
- Guests arrive by ferry, then ceremony/party at restaurant
- More casual approach
