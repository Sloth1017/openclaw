## 📋 Official Amsterdam Resources

### **Amsterdam Municipality Wedding Venues Directory**
- **URL:** https://www.amsterdam.nl/burgerzaken/trouwen-partnerschap/trouwlocaties-gemeente-amsterdam/
- **What it is:** Official City of Amsterdam registry of approved wedding venues
- **Why it matters:** Official certification, legal compliance, verified venue list
- **Use for:** Cross-reference venues, confirm municipality approval, find backup options

### **Storefront**

### **Toptrouwlocaties.nl**
- **URL:** toptrouwlocaties.nl
- **Type:** Wedding venue directory (filterable)
- **Best for:** Filter by style, location, price, capacity

### **DNLS.nl**
- **URL:** dnls.nl/trouwlocatie/amsterdam
- **Type:** 100+ Amsterdam wedding venues
- **Best for:** Comprehensive search + comparisons
