## ✅ DECISION FRAMEWORK

**When comparing venues, evaluate:**
- ✅ Date availability (Sept 19, 2026)
- ✅ Capacity (100 guests)
- ✅ Vibe (industrial? garden? historic? creative?)
- ✅ Inclusivity (venue + food + bar all-in? Or separate?)
- ✅ Ceremony option (can you have ceremony there, or separate?)
- ✅ Boat access (waterfront or ferry nearby?)
- ✅ Price (€17K-€25K range)
- ✅ Flexibility (can you customize? decor? music?)

---

## 📝 QUESTIONS FOR VENUES (WHEN COMPARING)

1. What's included in the price? (Venue only? Food? Bar?)
2. Can we bring our own DJ or must we use yours?
3. Are decorations included or do we bring our own?
4. What's the latest we can stay? (Last guest left time?)
5. Is there a separate ceremony space, or ceremony + reception in same space?
6. What's the payment schedule? (Deposit now, balance before? Installments?)
7. Can we do a site visit before final decision?
8. What's your cancellation policy?
9. Are there sound/noise restrictions? (Especially if Hangar — Beach Club issue!)
10. What's included in setup/breakdown?
