## UNIQUE WEDDING VENUES

### Museum Venues

#### Rijksmuseum ⭐
- **Location:** Museumplein
- **Vibe:** World-class art museum, historic grandeur
- **Spaces:** Various spaces including the courtyard with columns and fountains
- **Capacity:** Flexible
- **Why it's unique:** Getting married surrounded by Rembrandt and Vermeer
- **Website:** https://www.rijksmuseum.nl
- **Note:** Check if they host private weddings

#### Stedelijk Museum
- **Location:** Museumplein
- **Vibe:** Modern art museum, contemporary and stylish
- **Spaces:** Multiple event spaces with art collection access
- **Capacity:** Flexible
- **Why it's unique:** Modern art backdrop, very Instagram-worthy
- **Website:** https://www.stedelijk.nl/en/museum/venue-hire

---

### Historic & Unique Venues

#### Koepelkerk (Dome Church)
- **Location:** Near Dam Square
- **Vibe:** 17th-century domed church, premium venue
- **Features:** 17th-century domed ceiling, considered one of the best wedding venues in the Netherlands
- **Capacity:** Large
- **Why it's unique:** Historic grandeur, high-profile venue
- **Note:** Mentioned as hosting luxury brands and high-profile couples

#### Huize Frankendael
- **Location:** Amsterdam-Oost
- **Vibe:** Last remaining manor house from Amsterdam's summer house era
- **Features:** Grand dining rooms, rustic coach house, manicured gardens
- **Capacity:** Medium
- **Why it's unique:** Only surviving manor house, featured in bridal magazines
- **Website:** https://www.huizefrankendael.nl

## DUTCH WEDDING TRADITIONS

### Traditions to Consider

#### 1. The Wishing Tree (Wensboom)
- Guests write wishes on decorative leaves and hang them on a tree
- Beautiful keepsake after the wedding
- Perfect for photos

#### 2. Bruidstoet (Bridal Procession)
- Traditional ordering of family and guests in procession
- Can be adapted for modern weddings

#### 3. Dutch Wedding Cake (Trouwtaart)
- Traditional Dutch wedding cake is different from tiered British/American cakes
- Consider a Dutch-style cake or dessert table

#### 4. The Toast ("Op de bruid en bruidegom!")
- Dutch toasting tradition
- Consider Dutch beers/jenever for the toast

#### 5. Wedding Favors (Bedankjes)
- Traditional Dutch wedding favors
- Ideas: mini stroopwafels, tulip bulbs, Dutch chocolates

#### 6. Wooden Shoes (Klompen)
- Decorative wooden shoes as part of decor
- Some couples wear them for photos

## AMSTERDAM-SPECIFIC IDEAS

### Arrival & Transportation

#### 1. Arrive by Canal Boat
- Bride/groom arrive at venue by private saloon boat
- Guests can take canal boats between ceremony and reception
- Most romantic way to see Amsterdam

#### 2. Bike Wedding
- Dutch wedding tradition: decorate bikes with flowers
- Bride and groom ride through city
- Very Amsterdam, very Instagram-worthy

#### 3. Ferry to NDSM
- If using NDSM venue (IJver, NDSM Loods, Helling7, A Lab)
- Guests take the free ferry from Central Station
- Makes arrival an adventure

### Photo Locations

#### 1. Canal Ring (Grachtengordel)
- UNESCO World Heritage site
- Classic Amsterdam backdrop
- Best at golden hour

#### 2. Rijksmuseum
- Iconic "I amsterdam" letters area (if still there)
- Museum architecture
- Check if permits needed

#### 3. NDSM Wharf
- Street art and industrial backdrop
- Very different from canal photos
- Graffiti walls, cranes, waterfront

#### 4. Magere Brug (Skinny Bridge)
- Most romantic bridge in Amsterdam
- Beautiful at night with lights

#### 5. Jordaan Neighborhood
- Charming streets, canals, cafes
- Authentic Amsterdam feel

### Entertainment Ideas

#### 1. Dutch Folk Music
- Consider traditional Dutch musicians for part of the reception
- Or modern Dutch DJs

#### 2. Canal Cruise for Guests
- If not getting married on a boat, offer canal cruises as pre-wedding activity
- Great for out-of-town guests

#### 3. Bike Tour
- Organized bike tour for guests day before wedding
- Very Dutch experience

|------|-------------|---------------|

## FLOWERS & DECOR

### Dutch Flower Ideas

#### 1. Tulips
- Obviously Dutch!
- September is late for fresh tulip fields, but available as cut flowers
- Consider tulip bulbs as favors (plant in fall, bloom in spring)

#### 2. Other Dutch Flowers
- Delft Blue inspired arrangements (white and blue)
- Wildflower meadows style
- Greenhouse flowers (Noorderlicht venue has this vibe)

#### Florists to Research

- **Jolly Florist**: https://www.jollyflorist.com
- **Euroflorist**: https://www.euroflorist.nl
- Local Amsterdam florists (search "bloemist Amsterdam")

### Decor Ideas

#### Industrial Chic Elements
- Exposed bulbs and Edison lights
- Steel and wood combinations
- Vintage industrial furniture
- Greenery to soften industrial spaces

#### Amsterdam Touches
- Bicycle elements in decor
- Canal house miniatures as table numbers
- Maps of Amsterdam as table runners
- Delft blue accents
