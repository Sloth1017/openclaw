## ABOUT AMSTERDAM WEST

Amsterdam West (specifically the Westerpark area) is a **perfect location** for your wedding guests. Once home to Amsterdam's old gasworks, it's now a buzzing neighborhood with:
- Leafy parks and striking architecture
- Creative energy and cultural gems
- Bars, street-food stalls, live music
- Walking distance to Westergas (your potential venue)
- Easy tram connections to city center

## TRANSPORT FROM HOTELS TO WESTERGAS

### From Westerpark Area Hotels
- **Walking:** 5-15 minutes to Westergas
- **Perfect for:** Conscious Hotel Westerpark guests

### From City Center (Jordaan, Canal District)
- **Tram 10 or 14:** Direct to Westergas
- **Time:** 15-20 minutes
- **Cost:** ~€3.40 per ride

### From Central Station Area
- **Tram 14:** Direct to Westergas
- **Time:** 20-25 minutes
- **Alternative:** Taxi/Uber (€15-€25)

### From Amsterdam West (Sloterdijk)
- **Train to Central + Tram 14:** 30 minutes
- **Taxi/Uber:** €20-€35

## BOOKING TIPS FOR GUESTS

### When to Book
- **September is peak season** — book ASAP
- Wedding blocks: Reserve 6-12 months ahead
- Individual bookings: 3-6 months ahead

### Wedding Room Blocks
Contact hotels directly to ask about:
- Group discounts (10-20% off)
- Block of rooms held at reduced rate
- Cancellation policies
- Complimentary upgrades for bride/groom

### Areas to Recommend
1. **Westerpark** — Walking distance to venue
2. **Jordaan** — Charming, great restaurants, easy tram
3. **Canal District** — Classic Amsterdam, well-connected
4. **De Pijp** — Trendy, good value, direct tram

### Areas to Avoid for Westergas Wedding
- **Amsterdam-Noord** — Requires ferry + tram (complicated)
- **South (Zuid)** — Far from West, long tram ride
- **East (Oost)** — Not well-connected to West
