## OVERVIEW: BOAT OPTIONS FOR 100 GUESTS

For 100 guests, you'll likely need:
- **2-3 large saloon boats** (40-50 guests each), OR
- **1 large party ship** (100+ capacity), OR
- **Multiple smaller boats** in a fleet

---

## OPTION 1: SALOON BOATS (CLASSIC & ELEGANT)

### Flagship Amsterdam
- **Website:** https://flagshipamsterdam.com
- **Boat "Titanic":** Up to 75 passengers, 100% electric
- **Pricing:** From €250/hour (smaller boats)
- **For 100 guests:** Need 2 boats
- **Estimated Cost:** €500-€800/hour for fleet
- **Best for:** Elegant canal cruises, arrival/departure

### Amsterdamse Salonboot Rederij
- **Website:** https://www.amsterdamse-salonbootrederij.nl
- **Saloon Boat Elisabeth:** 2-12 persons, €230 first hour, €200 additional hours
- **Jean Schmitz:** Larger boat (1906 classic)
- **For 100 guests:** Need multiple boats
- **Best for:** Intimate groups, classic elegance

### Rederij Aemstelland
- **Website:** https://www.aemstelland.nl/en/events/
- **Features:** Classic salon boats, catering available
- **Services:** Weddings, dinner cruises, business events
- **Best for:** Full-service packages with catering

## USE CASES FOR YOUR WEDDING

### 1. Guest Transport (Ceremony → Reception)
**Best Option:** Multiple saloon boats or 1 large party ship
- Transport all guests between venues
- 30-60 minute canal cruise
- Drinks and music on board
- **Cost:** €1,000-€4,000

### 2. Bride & Groom Arrival
**Best Option:** Private luxury saloon boat
- Just the couple (or + wedding party)
- Dramatic entrance at venue
- Photos on the boat
- **Cost:** €300-€800

### 3. Pre-Wedding Activity
**Best Option:** Canal cruise for out-of-town guests
- Day before wedding
- Welcome activity
- **Cost:** €50-€100/person

### 4. Post-Ceremony Cruise
**Best Option:** Large party ship
- Ceremony on land, then cruise
- Dinner or drinks on board
- **Cost:** €2,000-€6,000

### 5. Full Wedding on Boat
**Best Option:** Wedding specialist company
- Ceremony, dinner, party all on boat
- **Cost:** €5,000-€15,000

## BOARDING LOCATIONS

### Central Amsterdam
1. **Pier 14** - Behind Central Station (most common)
2. **Zouthaven** - Near Muziekgebouw aan het IJ
3. **Danzigerkade** - Western dock area

### Amsterdam-Noord
4. **NDSM Wharf** - Perfect for your industrial venues
5. **EYE Filmmuseum** - Free ferry connection

### Near Your Venues
- **De Kromhouthal / Helling7:** IJ waterfront (direct boat access)
- **NDSM Venues:** NDSM Wharf (direct access)
- **Westergas:** No direct boat (landlocked in park)
- **Roest:** Near water but no dock

## SEPTEMBER WEATHER CONSIDERATIONS

**Pros:**
- Still relatively warm (15-20°C average)
- Less rain than October/November
- Long daylight hours

**Cons:**
- Can be unpredictable
- Evening might be chilly
- Have indoor backup plan

**Recommendations:**
- Choose boats with indoor/covered areas
- Provide blankets for guests
- Have umbrellas on standby
- Consider heated boats for evening

---

## QUESTIONS TO ASK BOAT COMPANIES

1. **Capacity:** Exact guest count per boat?
2. **Duration:** Minimum/maximum rental time?
3. **Route:** Can you customize the route?
4. **Boarding:** Where can guests board/disembark?
5. **Catering:** Can you bring your own food/drinks?
6. **Music:** Sound system available? DJ allowed?
7. **Decorations:** Can you decorate the boat?
8. **Weather:** What happens if it rains?
9. **Insurance:** Included in price?
10. **Cancellation:** Policy for bad weather?
