## Things to Ask When Booking Appointments

- [ ] How far in advance to order? (typically 6-9 months minimum)
- [ ] What's the price range?
- [ ] Do they do alterations in-house?
- [ ] Can you bring guests? (how many)
- [ ] Appointment length
- [ ] Cancellation/rescheduling policy
