## WHAT'S INCLUDED IN VENUE HIRE?

### Typically Included:
- Venue space rental
- Basic furniture (tables, chairs)
- Basic lighting
- Venue coordinator
- Cleaning fee

### Typically NOT Included:
- Catering (food & drinks)
- Bar service
- Decorations
- Flowers
- Music/DJ
- Photographer
- Wedding planner
- Insurance

### Always Ask:
- [ ] What's included in the venue hire?
- [ ] Is there a minimum spend requirement?
- [ ] Are there preferred caterers or can you bring your own?
- [ ] Is there a corkage fee for bringing your own alcohol?
- [ ] What are the setup/breakdown times?
- [ ] Is there a curfew?
- [ ] What's the cancellation policy?

## MONEY-SAVING TIPS

1. **Friday or Sunday Wedding** — Often 10-20% cheaper than Saturday
2. **Off-Peak Season** — November-March (but you're in September)
3. **Bring Your Own Caterer** — Some venues allow external catering
4. **Limit Bar Hours** — Open bar for limited time vs. all night
5. **DIY Decorations** — Industrial venues look great with minimal decor
6. **Digital Invitations** — Save on printing and postage
7. **Local Flowers** — Seasonal Dutch flowers vs. imported
8. **Student Musicians** — Conservatory students for ceremony music
