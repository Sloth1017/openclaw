# Wedding Venue Shortlist - Industrial Chic

**Wedding Date:** September 19, 2026  
**Location:** Amsterdam  
**Style:** Industrial Chic  

---

## Top Picks

### 1. Westergas / WestWeelde ⭐
- **Location:** Westerpark (Cultural District)
- **Boat accessible:** ❌ No (landlocked in park)
- **Vibe:** Former gas factory, historic industrial buildings, green park setting
- **Spaces:** Multiple venues including:
  - WesterUnie (industrial, large balcony)
  - WesterLiefde (intimate, ceremony + reception possible)
  - Gashouder (iconic circular space)
  - Machinegebouw
  - Transformatorhuis
- **Capacity:** Flexible - small to large weddings
- **Why it works:** Can do full wedding flow (ceremony → reception → dinner → party) in one spot
- **Website:** https://westergas.nl / https://westweelde.nl
- **Status:** ☐ Contact  ☐ Visit  ☐ Quote

---

### 2. De Kromhouthal ⭐
- **Location:** Amsterdam-Noord, on the IJ waterfront
- **Boat accessible:** ✅ Yes (directly on IJ waterfront)
- **Vibe:** Former engine factory, massive 5,000-6,000m² industrial hall
- **Features:** 15m high ceilings, original cranes, steel constructions, waterfront views of downtown/central station
- **Capacity:** Large (can be divided for smaller events)
- **Why it works:** Raw industrial grandeur, waterfront setting, very flexible layout
- **Website:** https://kromhouthal.com
- **Status:** ☐ Contact  ☐ Visit  ☐ Quote

### 3. Roest ⭐
- **Location:** Amsterdam-Oost (Eastern Docklands)
- **Boat accessible:** ❌ No (landlocked, near water but no dock)
- **Vibe:** Industrial bar & event space, recently reopened
- **Features:** 6 different spaces (grote zaal, wijnkamer, podium, keuken, studio, mezzanine), summer terrace, seats 200
- **Capacity:** 200 guests
- **Why it works:** Multiple spaces for different parts of the day, urban industrial feel
- **Website:** https://roest-amsterdam.nl
- **Status:** ☐ Contact  ☐ Visit  ☐ Quote
